Privacy & GDPR Notice

Welcome to Timing Challenge. This page explains how we handle your data and cookies in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Cookies Used by the Game

This game uses cookies to store your personal highscore locally in your browser. The cookies are:

  • timing_highscore: Stores your Normal mode highscore.
  • timing_highscore_ninja: Stores your Ninja mode highscore.

These cookies are strictly local and do not contain personal data such as your name or email.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Cookies are used to remember your highscore so that you can continue playing across sessions.
  • No personal identification data is collected by the game itself.
  • Score sharing via WhatsApp is optional and initiated by you. The game does not store or transmit your personal messages.

3. Sharing Data

The game does not automatically share any of your data with third parties. Sharing scores is entirely voluntary through the WhatsApp share button.

4. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view the highscores stored in your cookies.
  • Deletion: You can delete your highscores by clearing your browser cookies.
  • Withdrawal: You can stop using the game at any time to prevent further cookies from being stored.

5. How to Delete Cookies

You can delete your highscore cookies at any time in your browser settings. Look for "cookies" or "site data" in your browser preferences and remove cookies for pietrof.github.io.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this GDPR notice, you can contact the game developer at pietro@example.com.
